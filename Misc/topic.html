Human Enhancement

Human augmentation refers to attempts to overcome the current limitations of the human body temporarily or permanently through natural or artificial means.

With the rise of scientific physicalism, scientists believe that it is feasible to use technology to continuously improve the human organism, because if humans are composed of matter and follow the same physical laws, then technology can be used to manipulate human properties in principle.

In the era of modern emerging technologies, human enhancement technology is constantly intervening and shaping people themselves. According to the purpose of enhancement, human enhancement can be divided into four categories: physiological enhancement, cognitive enhancement, moral enhancement and compound enhancement.

Why Human Enhancement is important?

Human enhancement is not only to treat diseases and disabilities, but also to enhance the quality of all aspects of human beings. The human enhancement also means emerging technologies and convergence technologies.

>>The human enhancement integrates nanotechnology, biotechnology, information technology and cognitive science to improve human performance.

>>Human enhancement technology is committed to the development of cognitive enhancement, physiological enhancement.

>>In medicine, he is committed to the treatment of diseases and disabilities, nerve implants and brain-computer interface technology.
